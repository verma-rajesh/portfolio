Hi, I'm Niki! Thanks for stopping by!

Here are some things you should know about me:

1. I’m a former dietitan who hates to cook.

What does this mean? Well, I didn’t get into nutrition for the food. I got into it because of the people. I didn’t go down the typical path as a dieitian. I have always been interested in psychology and helping those who didn’t know how to help themselves. This led me to my enriching work as a nutritional counselor with the eating disorder population. By creating a safe space for my patients to share their stories, I provided realistic solutions that empowered them to enact change within themselves.

2. Bonjour! I’m a Francophile.

I’ve traveled all over the world but have a stong affinity for the French people and their culture. I spent a few months learning the language and getting to know the people. I find the French have an eye for style and design, and they are not afraid to push perceived fashion limitations. I find joy in this visual culture and have used it to inspire my own designs: minimilistic with an edge.

3. I am a HUGE Harry Potter fan..

As a designer, it is important to be able to paint a story for others. The Harry Potter series still to this day invokes vivid and imaginative imagery (yes, I re-read this series often). I still feel elated when Harry gets his letter to Hogwarts; I feel pain when Dobby dies, and I feel hope as each year the three friends defeat Voldermort. Painting a picture, sketching an idea, and evoking a feeling are all things that are neccessary as an effective UX designer.
